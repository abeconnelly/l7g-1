Lightning is suite of programs for rapid exploration of populations of genomic data. This page points some of the underlying components.

Please see our preprint for more details on Lightning and the compact representation. Living documents and other details can be found in the l7g repository.

All projects are under AGPLv3 and all data is available CC0, unless otherwise noted.
